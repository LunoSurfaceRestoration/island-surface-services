About Us

Luno Surface Restoration is a small, locally owned business dedicated to high-quality residential surface care. We specialize in power washing, concrete sealing, asphalt crack sealing, and jet sealing to restore and protect your driveways, patios, and walkways. Based in Victoria, BC, we take pride in helping our community keep their homes clean, protected, and looking great.

Our Services

Power Washing

High-pressure cleaning to remove dirt, oil, moss, and grime from concrete and paved surfaces.

Concrete Sealing

Protective gloss sealing to shield your concrete from weathering, stains, and cracking.

Asphalt Crack Sealing

Sealing cracks to prevent water damage and extend the life of your asphalt driveway.

Jet Sealing

Durable and flexible sealing method that enhances appearance and resistance to wear.
